Shaping the Future of Information and Data

Welcome to the College of Information, Data and Society! Previously known as the College of Professional and Global Education, our name reflects the academic units within the college: the School of Information (iSchool) and the Department of Applied Data Science (ADS). This connection highlights the significant impact of these departments on society. The College of Information, Data and Society offers interdisciplinary and forward-looking academic programs that prepare students to address the world’s increasing challenges around analyzing, managing, protecting, securing, designing, organizing and preserving data, information, records and archives through technology-oriented, user-focused practices and solutions.

Dean's Welcome

Academic Programs

Department of Applied Data Science

The Department of Applied Data Science offers academic programs tailored to the workforce needs for interdisciplinary talents in designing and deploying intelligent solutions to real-world data challenges. Over 150 Silicon Valley companies, including industry giants like Amazon, Tesla, Meta Platforms and Uber, have hired MSADI students for internships and permanent roles. This extensive network provides invaluable connections and inspiration for students entering dynamic tech fields.

Explore our MS in Applied Data Intelligence Program

School of Information

The San José State University School of Information (iSchool) prepares individuals for careers as information professionals (including in librarianship, informatics, archives and records management, data analysis, information science and teacher librarianship), offering various bachelor's and master's degrees programs, doctoral partnership programs, certifications and credential programs.

Bridging Knowledge Scholars Celebrated at Closing Ceremony

The School of Information at SJSU celebrated the Bridging Knowledge Scholars Closing Ceremony this spring, honoring Indigenous students pursuing careers in libraries, archives, and museums. The event highlighted the power of mentorship, community, and Indigenous leadership in shaping the future of the information profession.

iSchool Promotes Jason Kaltenbacher to Associate Director

The San José State University School of Information is excited to announce that Jason Kaltenbacher has been promoted to associate director! Jason has been a dedicated lecturer, program coordinator, and leader within the iSchool, bringing expertise in informatics, records management, and information governance. In his new role, he looks forward to supporting our faculty, staff, and programs as the iSchool continues to grow.

Read the announcement

SJSU College of IDS Announces Leadership Transition

The College of IDS announces exciting leadership changes. After 15 years of service, Dr. Sandy Hirsh has stepped down as Associate Dean of Academics to begin a new role as Special Assistant to the Provost for AI Initiatives. Dr. Tonia San Nicolas-Rocca has been appointed Interim Associate Dean of Academics, bringing her expertise in cybersecurity, AI, and student success to the position.

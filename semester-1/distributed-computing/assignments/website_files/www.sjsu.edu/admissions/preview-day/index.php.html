SJSU Fall Preview Day

Fall Preview Day (October 11)

Welcome to SJSU Fall Preview Day! Were excited to have you explore our vibrant campus, connect with faculty and students, and discover what it means to be a Spartan. Enjoy tours, workshops, presentations, and a glimpse into your future at San Jos State University!

Fall Preview Day Registration

Registration for this event is now open!

Register Now

Mobile-friendly Schedule of Events

College Presentations

Attend our college presentations featuring every academic college at SJSU. Meet and learn from faculty representing the different majors, programs, resources and careers that you are interested in. Engage with representatives and learn what awaits you in their department as a future Spartan.

Special Topic Workshops and Open Houses

Join us for an exciting array of special topic workshops such as Financial Aid and an Admissions and Cal State Apply presentation. Meet our dedicated team from our Career Center to learn about internships and job exploration.

Campus Tours

Embark on a tour of our vibrant 154-acre campus, guided by our experienced SJSU student ambassadors.

Meet at Tower Lawn to join one of our many campus tour offerings. Tours are offered on a first come first serve basis. We look forward to meeting you!

University Housing

Meet our University Housing Services staff and learn about the many offerings for campus housing at SJSU. Take a virtual tour of our residence halls, learn about meal plans and expected costs, and participate in a Q&A with our staff.

Visit the Special Topic Workshops page for details.

*Please note: Due to current occupancy levels, in-person tours of housing units will not be available.

Spartan East Side Promise

The Spartan East Side Promise Program (SESP) is SJSU's dedicated support service for East Side Union High School District students from the time of college exploration through college graduation. 

Spartan Resource Fair

Dont miss the Spartan Resource Fair from 9 a.m. to 12:30 p.m., where you can connect with over 100 academic departments, services, organizations, and campus resources at this large-scale event on the Paseo.

Spartan Bookstore

Be sure to drop into the Spartan Bookstore to stock up on SJSU apparel and show your school pride!

*Stop by the bookstore table during the fair to learn more about what we offerand pick up your exclusive coupon for the campus bookstore! Dont miss out on savings and school spirit.

Spartan Eats Dining

Grab a bite to eat during Fall Preview Day with one of our campus dining partners!

Event Parking

Parking details were included in your registration confirmation email. Check out the Event Parking page for details!

For general parking information, please visit the Parking Services page or contact them at 408-924-6557 or via email at parking@sjsu.edu

Event Map

Check out our Fall Preview Day event map to help you get around campus with ease!

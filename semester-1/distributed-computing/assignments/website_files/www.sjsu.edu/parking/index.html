News and Announcements

Current Parking Impacts

Parking in main campus parking garages can be difficult and will add delays to your day between 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. Parking Services encourages parking at the South Campus Parking Garage and taking the free and frequent shuttle to Main Campus.  Another option for students and employees is to use a VTA EcoPass loaded onto a Clipper Card for public transportation throughout Santa Clara County. Find out more at go.sjsu.edu/transportation

As a reminder, there is no grace period for parking. All users of any university parking garage must immediately have a valid parking permit or use the Parkmobile app to pay. 

Parking Permit News

  • The cost of employee campus parking permits for CSUEU, SUPA, UAPD, C99, E99, MPP and Auxiliary employees will increase beginning July 1, 2025, due to approved employee rate changes. The one-time increase will be $2 for the monthly employee permit rate, with a similar percentage increase applied to all other employee permit types. For additional information, visit Employee Parking Permit page.
  • The cost of student campus parking permits increased on July 1, 2025. For additional information, visit Commuter Student Parking Permit page or Housing Student Parking Permit page.
  • The cost of visitor parking permits increased on July 1, 2025.
  • Employees: get your annual permit through the Parking Online Services website. 
  • Parking permits for the South Campus Parking Garage are valid in general spaces of the main campus parking garages only between 4:30 p.m. to 11:59 p.m. on Mondays through Thursdays, and from 7 a.m. to 11:59 p.m. on Fridays through Sundays. However, no South Campus Parking Garage permits are valid for overnight parking at Main Campus garages. Overnight permits are only offered to students living in university housing (on-campus and off-campus sites: International House and Spartan Village on the Paseo).

General News

  • Pay via ParkMobile app (Zone 9033) or Permit Pay-Stations available on the first floor by the elevators. 
  • Parking citation fees are being adjusted to better support parking operations and your parking experience. Fees were adjusted starting August 1, 2025.  These changes reflect increased costs associated with enforcement operations, state-mandated operations, state-mandated surcharges, and continued support in campus transportation services.  We appreciate your cooperation as we continue improving campus mobility and safety. 

General Information

About Us

We are here to help students, faculty, staff and visitors park in a safe and clean environment. We maintain 7,500 parking spaces to help our campus function safely and efficiently, and provide other crucial services for the community.

Parking Permits

We know that the SJSU community has diverse needs when it comes to parking. That's why we offer a variety of parking permit types at various locations that can last anywhere from a single day to a semester.

Parking Citations

Make sure you stay up to date on all our regulations, which are enforced 24-hours a day, 7 days a week, every day, including holidays. It's also important to note that if you don't follow our regulations, you could be subject to a citation or fine.

Parking Apps

All visitors can pay for parking through the Parkmobile app to extend parking or get reminders.

How We Can Help

Have a question about where you can park, our parking permits, citations or anything parking-related? We're here to help!
